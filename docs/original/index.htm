About Insyte
Innovative Systems Techniques (Insyte) designs and installs database management and decision support systems using VisionTM, our object-oriented database technology. As both a technology developer and a systems implementer, we uniquely offer clients access to our Vision technology and the direct involvement of our highly capable staff. We have installed mission-critical, production systems for clients in such industries as investment management, international trade, health care, and media research. Find out more by visiting About Us.

Vision... Ahead-of-the-art
Vision, our analytical database management technology, has been the cornerstone of our clients' successful production systems for over ten years. Vision is object-oriented, yet its heritage originates from database management theory and practice. It offers unmatched capabilities in storing, manipulating, and analyzing large, complex, time-sensitive databases. Find out more by visiting About Us. Find out a lot more by visiting Vision Online, our product documentation.
Modeling Time-Varying Data
Time represents one dimension in our multi-dimensional world. Because different points in time reflect different states of the world, a database dealing with time-varying data needs to do the same. Find out how object oriented technology and Vision in particular handle the complexity of time in our white paper, Time Is Not A Time-Series.
