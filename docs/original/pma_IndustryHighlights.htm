Application: IndustryHighlights

 

Summary:

The IndustryHighlights application is used to display summary financial information about an industry over time.

Inputs:

Position Form Id Description 
1  id  Id of the industry 

Analysis:

  • The id must reference an existing Industry instance.
  • If no global currency has been specified, all financial data is coverted to US dollars. Fiscal year data is accessed as of the year end on or before the evaluation date.
  • The basic report displays the last 3 years of the following statistics:
    • Number of Companies in Industry
    Total Industry Sales
    Company in Industry with Largest Sales
    Total Industry Market Capitalization
    Company in Industry with Largest Market Cap
    Average Industry P/E ratio
    Company in Industry with Largest P/E
    Company in Industry with Smallest P/E
  • This report links to a pie charts by sales and market cap, and to a bar chart by pe.

Key Data Feeds:

  • FundamentalA
  • FundamentalM
  • CompanyToIndustry

Related Reports:

  • IndustryProfile

Sample Usage:

    Interface ApplicationWS runWith: "IndustryHighlights", "530" ;
